## Scope
Hi,

We really like the overall design and user experience of this website:

https://hotelarena.nl/en/new-park-cafe-restaurant/

We are not looking for an exact copy, but we would like a similar premium, modern and clean look.

Our website should primarily focus on the restaurant, with high-quality images, an easy table reservation system and a welcoming atmosphere.

In addition, we would like to include:

* Table Reservations
* Holiday Home Enquiry
* Jacuzzi Reservations
* Bike Reservations
* Event Reservations
* Gallery
* Contact & Opening Hours
* Dutch, English and German language support

We already have a website (www.oudkempeneetcafe.nl), so please use the existing content and improve the design and functionality.

One thing I’d change from Hotel Arena

I wouldn’t hide the extra services in the menu. I’d put them directly on the homepage as attractive cards after the restaurant section, for example:

🍽️ Reserve a Table

🚲 Bike Rental

🛁 Jacuzzi Rental

🏡 Holiday Home Enquiry

🎉 Host Your Event

That way, visitors immediately see everything Oud-Kempen offers without taking attention away from the restaurant. I think that’s the ideal balance for your business.
